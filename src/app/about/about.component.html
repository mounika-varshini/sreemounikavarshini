SSC

2016 was the year I wrote my first board exams, SSC (Out of all the possibilities they had, they choose "Secondary School Certificate"). Anyways, my results were a total bummer and didn't live up to my expectations or my family's. I scored 9.2/10, so not too shabby after all. Despite my grade, the real value was in the sick skills and the knowledge I picked up through the process.BTW, I had the pleasure of gracing the halls of Sri Sahiti High School, Sangareddy during my schooling!

Intermediate

I bid farewell to my high school days and joined the chaos at Sri Chaitanya Junior College, Hyderabad in the stream of MPC- it was like stepping into a mini jail(yes it's not a myth). Nevertheless, I aced it with a mind-blowing 96.3%.Looking back, it was one heck of a ride, and I'm proud to have made it out with both my brain and my sanity still functioning (mostly).
